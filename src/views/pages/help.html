Help & About

Welcome to the Fashion Image Database for 'Sociology of Fashion'

Encounters

Encounters are the main category of data. An encounter represents an instance of you meeting with, photographing and recording the relevant details of a research subject (person).

Theme

Choose from the existing themes or make your own.

Tags

You can apply upto 5 tags. These represent characteristics of the subject which they may share with others.

Clothing

Add details about the items of interest.

Images

The most important bit! First save the Encouter details. The subject agreement must have been ticked. After saving, you can add images. Tap the icon in the images section, then use the upload or capture buttons to upload or take+upload images. After uploading, you can manage the images and add a detail comment to any of them or delete any you no longer require.
